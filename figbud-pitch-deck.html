Business Line 3 · Building Month 1–2 · Live Month 2–3

AI CCTV —
Security-as-a-Service

Target market: SMEs, schools, banks and security firms — entirely separate from the ISP residential market. Cameras run on Starlink connectivity. AI detects anomalies. Clients get WhatsApp alerts and live mobile feeds.

⚠
Infrastructure Build — Months 1–2
CCTV infrastructure is under construction. Camera hardware, AI platform, and billing are being configured during Month 1–2. First client sites go live Month 2. Full commercial rollout Month 3.
01
Camera Hardware Procurement
Dahua IPC-HFW2849S 4MP IR cameras sourced and bench-tested. Site spec locked: 4 cameras (SME/school), 8–16 (bank/estate). Each camera runs AI motion and intrusion detection at the edge — no cloud dependency for alerts.
02
AI Platform & Alert Pipeline
Dahua DSS Pro NVR with AI Smart Detection module configured — perimeter intrusion, loitering, face detection. Alerts delivered via Africa's Talking SMS API and WhatsApp Business API. False-alert suppression tuned before client go-live.
03
Client Portal & NOC Integration
Clients access live feeds and alert history via Dahua DMSS mobile app (iOS + Android). All CCTV sites added to the same Zabbix NOC monitoring instance used for ISP — one pane of glass for all infrastructure.
04
M-Pesa Subscription Billing Live
Monthly CCTV subscription collected via Safaricom Daraja API — same autopay system as ISP. Non-payment triggers automatic camera suspension at 72 hours. Reactivation instant on payment.
